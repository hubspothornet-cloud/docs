## Should you buy a dragon boat paddle as a gift?

A dragon boat paddle can be a premium gift, but it is best when you know the paddler's preferred handle option, length needs, race requirements, and design preference. If you do not know those details, choose accessories first.

<Warning>
  Do not buy a premium paddle only by color or artwork unless you know the paddler's fit and handle needs. Paddle specs and approval requirements matter more than design.
</Warning>

## FAQs

### What is a good small gift for a dragon boat paddler?

Dragon Guard, silicone grip tape, and paddle cleaner are good small Hornet gift options because they support paddle protection, grip, and care without requiring exact paddle sizing.

### What is the safest dragon boat gift if I do not know the paddler's size?

Accessories are safer than paddles when you do not know fit details. Start with a seat pad, Dragon Guard, grip tape, paddle cleaner, blade cover, or paddle bag.

### Is a dragon boat paddle a good gift?

A dragon boat paddle can be a great premium gift if you know the paddler's preferred handle, length needs, race requirements, and design preference. Otherwise, accessories are safer.

### What gift should I buy for a dragon boat team?

For teams, consider practical accessories such as seat pads, Dragon Guards, silicone grip tape, and paddle cleaner. These are easier to buy in quantity than individual paddles.
