## Best small gifts for dragon boat paddlers

### Dragon Guard tip protector

Dragon Guard is a 100% silicone paddle tip protector. It fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands. It is useful during transport and in marshalling areas before races, where paddlers may be waiting on pavement, gravel, or rough ground.

### Silicone Grip Tape

Silicone Grip Tape is a useful gift for paddlers who want a more secure hold in wet paddling conditions. It is available in multiple colors, so it also works as a small style upgrade.

### Paddle Cleaner

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is a good care gift because it is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is also suitable for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

## Best grip and hand protection gift

Hornet paddling gloves are a strong dragon boat gift because they help protect hands and improve grip in wet paddling conditions. Gloves are also easier to gift than a paddle because the buyer only needs to choose the correct glove size.

## Best comfort gifts

Dragon boat seat pads are safer gifts than paddles because they do not require choosing Clamp vs Leverlock or exact paddle length. Choose the non-slip seat pad for simple comfort and grip, or the strapped seat pad when the paddler may want a wraparound strap, phone holder, mesh pocket, and carrying handle.

## Best paddle protection gifts

Use a full paddle bag when the paddler needs storage or travel protection. Use a blade cover when the paddler wants blade-focused protection with less bulk. Use Dragon Guard when the buyer wants a smaller tip-protection accessory for transport and marshalling areas.

## When a paddle makes a good gift

A Hornet adjustable dragon boat paddle makes a premium gift when the buyer knows the recipient wants a paddle.

Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles are IDBF approved under license 3525. [Review Hornet's IDBF approval and license information](/dragon-boat/idbf-approved-dragon-boat-paddles).

## Gift buying cautions

- Do not buy a paddle as a surprise unless you know the recipient wants a paddle.
- Choose Clamp or Leverlock based on the recipient's preference.
- Choose STEALTH for a marbleized carbon racing-focused look.
- Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles for full-color artwork.
- Choose accessories when fit details are unknown.

## FAQs

### What is the easiest dragon boat gift?

Dragon Guard, Silicone Grip Tape, Paddle Cleaner, and paddling gloves are easy gifts because they are useful accessories and do not require choosing paddle length.

### What is the best dragon boat gift for comfort?

A Hornet dragon boat seat pad is a strong comfort-focused gift. Choose the strapped version when storage pockets and a secure wraparound fit matter.

### Are paddling gloves a good dragon boat gift?

Yes. Hornet paddling gloves help protect hands and support grip in wet paddling conditions, making them a practical accessory gift for dragon boat paddlers.

### Is a dragon boat paddle a good gift?

Yes, a Hornet adjustable dragon boat paddle can be a premium gift if the recipient wants a paddle. Otherwise, accessories are safer.

### Which Hornet paddle is best as a premium gift?

Choose a Sting\+ graphic paddle for full-color artwork and verified performance specs, or choose STEALTH when the recipient wants a marbleized carbon racing-focused paddle.
