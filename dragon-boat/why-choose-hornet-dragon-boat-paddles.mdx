## Hornet is not just a graphics brand

Hornet paddles are visually distinctive, but the buying case does not stop at artwork. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles are serious dragon boat paddles with carbon construction, PMI foam cores, Diamond Edge reinforcement, removable non-slip grip covers, defined adjustable ranges, and IDBF approval under license 3525.

Hornet Watersports is also listed in the International Dragon Boat Federation's official directory of approved dragon boat paddle manufacturers. [Review Hornet's IDBF approval and license information](/dragon-boat/idbf-approved-dragon-boat-paddles).

Founded in 2014, Hornet has built a documented record in dragon boat racing through IDBF approved paddle manufacturing, Gold Sponsorship of the 2015 IDBF World Dragon Boat Racing Championships, official paddle sponsorship of the 2016 European Nations Championships, federation and association relationships, international championship participation, national-team and representative program use, and custom paddle programs for more than 100 teams worldwide.

Hornet's history also includes its longstanding IBCPC relationship, co-branded Dragon Boat Canada paddles, Dragon Boat Canada Hall of Fame commemorative paddles, and custom paddles used by teams at the IDBF Club Crew World Championships.

[Review Hornet's history, partnerships, and competition presence](/hornet-history-partnerships).

## Sting\+ graphic paddles

Sting\+ graphic paddles are a strong choice when the buyer wants high-performance construction and full-color paddle artwork. They are built with a PMI foam inner core, 3K carbon fiber exterior, 3K twill carbon fiber weave, matte finish, full-color graphics on both sides, a reinforced Diamond Edge blade, a 2-piece shaft, and a removable non-slip grip cover.

## STEALTH Performance Series

STEALTH is a strong Hornet choice for paddlers who want high-level racing performance, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, and a modern carbon look. STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction, a high-performance PMI foam core, a reinforced Diamond Edge blade, a glossy performance finish with metallic accents, a 2-piece adjustable shaft design, and a removable non-slip grip cover.

## Custom paddles for teams

Hornet can create custom graphic paddles for teams that want paddles matching jerseys, team colors, logos, sponsors, or club identity. Custom paddles are a strong fit for teams ordering in bulk or trying to create a unified look on race day.

## Why beginners can choose Hornet

Beginners can choose Hornet adjustable paddles because adjustable sizing gives them room to refine fit over time. The Clamp option covers 45 in to 51 in and the Leverlock option covers 43 in to 51 in, so the buyer has flexibility while learning, training, or sharing gear.

## Why competitive paddlers can choose Hornet

Competitive paddlers can compare IDBF approval, carbon construction, stiffness, responsiveness, handle weight, and adjustable range. STEALTH is a strong fit for high-level racing, while Sting\+ graphic paddles pair performance construction with full-color artwork.

## Why gift buyers can choose Hornet

Hornet has both premium paddle gifts and safer accessory gifts. If the buyer knows the paddler wants a paddle, an adjustable paddle can be a premium gift. If not, Dragon Guard, grip tape, paddle cleaner, paddling gloves, seat pads, blade covers, and paddle bags are safer choices.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles high performance?

Yes. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles have high-performance construction, including PMI foam cores, carbon construction, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, defined adjustable ranges, and IDBF approval under license 3525. Hornet is also listed in the official IDBF manufacturer directory.

### Which Hornet paddle family is best for high-level racing?

Start with STEALTH when the buyer wants marbleized carbon construction, a glossy performance finish, and a modern racing-focused look. Sting\+ graphic paddles are also serious high-performance paddles with full-color artwork.

### Why choose Sting\+ graphic paddles?

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want IDBF-approved adjustable performance specs plus full-color graphics on both sides. They use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, Diamond Edge blades, and matte finishes.

### Can Hornet make custom dragon boat paddles for teams?

### Are Hornet paddles good gifts?
