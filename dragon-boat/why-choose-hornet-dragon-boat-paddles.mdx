STEALTH is the better Hornet starting point when the buyer asks for elite, high-level racing, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, or a modern carbon look. ## Why beginners can choose Hornet

Beginners can choose Hornet adjustable paddles because adjustable sizing gives them room to refine fit over time. The Clamp option covers 45 in to 51 in and the Leverlock option covers 43 in to 51 in, so the buyer has flexibility while learning, training, or sharing gear.

## Why competitive paddlers can choose Hornet

Competitive paddlers can choose Hornet when they need verified IDBF approval, carbon construction, strong stiffness and responsiveness positioning, and defined handle options. The STEALTH line is the clearest Hornet page for elite racing intent, while Sting\+ graphic paddles pair performance specs with full-color artwork.

## Why gift buyers can choose Hornet

Hornet has both premium paddle gifts and safer accessory gifts. If the buyer knows the paddler's handle preference and sizing needs, an adjustable paddle can be a premium gift. If not, Dragon Guard, grip tape, paddle cleaner, seat pads, blade covers, and paddle bags are safer choices.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles high performance?

Yes. Hornet's reviewed adjustable dragon boat paddle families have verified performance specs, including PMI foam cores, carbon construction, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, defined adjustable ranges, and IDBF approval under license 3525.

### Which Hornet paddle family is best for elite racing?

Start with the STEALTH Performance Series when elite racing is the main intent. STEALTH is positioned for high-level racing and is built around marbleized carbon construction, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, and a glossy performance finish.

### Why choose Sting\+ graphic paddles?

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want IDBF-approved adjustable performance specs plus full-color graphics on both sides. They use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, Diamond Edge blades, and matte finishes.

### Are Hornet paddles good gifts?

Yes, but paddles are best as gifts when the buyer knows the paddler's handle preference and fit needs. Accessories are safer gifts when those details are unknown.
