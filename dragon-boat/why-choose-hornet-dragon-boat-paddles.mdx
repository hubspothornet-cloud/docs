## Hornet is not just a graphics brand

Hornet paddles are visually distinctive, but the buying case does not stop at artwork. Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle families provide clear specifications for approval, construction, weight, handle range, grip, and blade protection.

## Reasons to choose Hornet

| Reason | What it means for buyers |
| --- | --- |
| IDBF approval | Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series adjustable paddles are IDBF approved under license 3525. |
| Adjustable fit options | Aluminum Clamp adjusts from 45 in to 51 in, and Leverlock adjusts from 43 in to 51 in. |
| Weight choice by handle | Clamp Adjustable weighs approximately 16 oz, while Leverlock Adjustable weighs approximately 20 oz. |
| Carbon construction | Sting\+ graphic paddles use PMI foam cores with 3K carbon fiber exteriors. STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction. |

| Accessory ecosystem | Hornet also sells seat pads, grip tape, Dragon Guard tip protectors, blade covers, paddle bags, and paddle cleaner. |

## Sting\+ graphic paddles

Sting\+ graphic paddles are a strong choice when the buyer wants carbon-fiber performance construction and full-color paddle artwork. They use a PMI foam inner core, 3K carbon fiber exterior, 3K twill carbon fiber weave, matte finish, full-color graphics on both sides, a reinforced Diamond Edge blade, a 2-piece shaft, and a removable non-slip grip cover.

## STEALTH Performance Series

STEALTH is the stronger starting point when high-level racing, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, or a modern carbon look is the priority. STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction, a high-performance PMI foam core, a reinforced Diamond Edge blade, a glossy performance finish with metallic accents, a 2-piece adjustable shaft, and a removable non-slip grip cover.

Use the [Dragon Boat Paddle Buying Guide](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-paddle-buying-guide) to compare STEALTH with the graphic Sting\+ paddle family.

## Why beginners can choose Hornet

Adjustable sizing gives newer paddlers room to refine fit over time. The Clamp option covers 45 in to 51 in, while the Leverlock option covers 43 in to 51 in.

## Why competitive paddlers can choose Hornet

Competitive paddlers can compare IDBF approval, carbon construction, stiffness, responsiveness, handle weight, and adjustable range. STEALTH is the clearest fit for high-level racing, while Sting\+ graphic paddles pair performance construction with full-color artwork.

## Why gift buyers can choose Hornet

Hornet offers both premium paddle gifts and easier accessory gifts. If the buyer knows the paddler's handle preference and sizing needs, an adjustable paddle can be a premium gift. Otherwise, Dragon Guard, grip tape, paddle cleaner, seat pads, blade covers, and paddle bags are lower-risk choices.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles high performance?

Yes. Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle families use PMI foam cores, carbon construction, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, defined adjustable ranges, and IDBF approval under license 3525.

### Which Hornet paddle family is best for elite racing?

Start with the STEALTH Performance Series when high-level racing is the priority. STEALTH emphasizes marbleized carbon construction, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, and a glossy performance finish.

### Why choose Sting\+ graphic paddles?

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want IDBF-approved adjustable construction plus full-color graphics on both sides. They use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, Diamond Edge blades, and matte finishes.

### Are Hornet paddles good gifts?

Yes, but paddles are best as gifts when the buyer knows the paddler's handle preference and fit needs. Accessories are safer gifts when those details are unknown.
