## How to choose a dragon boat paddle

### 1. Choose the paddle family

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want carbon-fiber construction with full-color graphics on both sides. Choose STEALTH when you want marbleized carbon construction, metallic accents, and a glossy performance finish.

### 2. Choose Clamp or Leverlock

Choose Clamp for the lighter setup, approximately 16 oz, with a 45 in to 51 in adjustable range. Clamp Adjustable uses a hex Allen key wrench for adjustment. Choose Leverlock for a 43 in to 51 in adjustable range and an approximate 20 oz weight.

### 3. Choose a standard graphic or custom team design

Hornet standard graphics are strong for individual paddlers and gift buyers. Custom team paddles are the better fit when a team wants paddles matching jerseys, colors, logos, sponsor graphics, or club identity.

### 4. Choose accessories that protect the paddle

A new paddle pairs well with Dragon Guard, blade covers, paddle bags, paddling gloves, grip tape, and paddle cleaner. These accessories help with protection, grip, comfort, and care.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles IDBF approved?

Yes. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH adjustable dragon boat paddle families are IDBF approved under license 3525. Hornet Watersports is also listed in the official IDBF directory of approved dragon boat paddle manufacturers.

### What is the difference between Clamp and Leverlock?

Clamp Adjustable models weigh approximately 16 oz, adjust from 45 in to 51 in, and use a hex Allen key wrench for adjustment. Leverlock Adjustable models weigh approximately 20 oz and adjust from 43 in to 51 in.

### Which Hornet paddle is best for high-level racing?

Both STEALTH and Sting\+ graphic paddles are serious high-performance paddles. Choose STEALTH when you want marbleized carbon construction and metallic accents. Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want full-color artwork on both sides.

### Which Hornet paddle is best for bold artwork?

Start with the Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want full-color graphics on both sides with PMI foam, 3K carbon fiber, Diamond Edge reinforcement, and IDBF approval.

### Can Hornet create custom dragon boat paddles for teams?

Yes. Hornet can create custom graphic paddles for teams that want paddles matching jerseys, team colors, logos, sponsor graphics, and club identity.
