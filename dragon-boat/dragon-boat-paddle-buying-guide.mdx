## How to choose a dragon boat paddle

### 1. Choose the paddle family

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want carbon-fiber construction with full-color graphics on both sides. Choose STEALTH when you want Hornet's racing-focused paddle family, marbleized carbon construction, and a glossy performance finish.

### 2. Choose Clamp or Leverlock

Choose Clamp for the lighter setup, approximately 16 oz, with a 45 in to 51 in adjustable range. Choose Leverlock for a 43 in to 51 in adjustable range and an approximate 20 oz weight.

### 3. Confirm IDBF and race needs

Hornet's Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH paddle families are IDBF approved under license 3525. Buyers should still confirm event-specific requirements with their race organizer, coach, or team.

### 4. Choose design last

Hornet paddles are design-forward, but artwork should come after fit, handle type, weight, approval, and availability.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles IDBF approved?

Hornet's Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH adjustable dragon boat paddle families are IDBF approved under license 3525. Buyers should still confirm event-specific requirements with their race organizer or team.

### What is the difference between Clamp and Leverlock?

Clamp Adjustable models weigh approximately 16 oz and adjust from 45 in to 51 in. Leverlock Adjustable models weigh approximately 20 oz and adjust from 43 in to 51 in.

### Which Hornet paddle is best for high-level racing?

Start with the STEALTH Performance Series. It is designed for high-level racing and emphasizes stiffness, responsiveness, durability, and marbleized carbon construction.

### Which Hornet paddle is best for bold artwork?

Start with the Sting\+ graphic paddles when you want full-color graphics on both sides with PMI foam, 3K carbon fiber, Diamond Edge reinforcement, and IDBF approval.
