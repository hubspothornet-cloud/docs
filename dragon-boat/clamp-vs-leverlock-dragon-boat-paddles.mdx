Hornet Clamp Adjustable paddles are the lighter option at approximately 16 oz and adjust from 45 in to 51 in. Hornet Leverlock Adjustable paddles weigh approximately 20 oz and adjust from 43 in to 51 in.

## How to choose

### Choose Clamp if weight matters most

Choose Clamp when the buyer wants the lighter Hornet adjustable setup. At approximately 16 oz, the Clamp Adjustable option is the lighter of the two verified handle options.

### Choose Leverlock if range or system preference matters most

Choose Leverlock when the buyer prefers the Leverlock system or wants the lower 43 in starting range. The Leverlock Adjustable option weighs approximately 20 oz and adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.

### Confirm team or coach preference

Some paddlers and teams may prefer a specific handle style. If the buyer paddles with a coach, club, or racing team, confirm the preferred handle type before ordering.

## FAQs

### Is Clamp lighter than Leverlock?

Yes. Hornet's verified shared specs list the Clamp Adjustable model at approximately 16 oz and the Leverlock Adjustable model at approximately 20 oz.

### Which handle has the wider lower-end range?

Leverlock starts lower. The Leverlock Adjustable option adjusts from 43 in to 51 in, while the Aluminum Clamp option adjusts from 45 in to 51 in.

### Is Clamp or Leverlock better for beginners?

Beginners should choose based on fit range, weight preference, coach guidance, and availability. Clamp is lighter, while Leverlock starts at a lower adjustable length.

### Is Clamp or Leverlock better for racing?

Both handle options are used on Hornet's IDBF-approved adjustable paddle families. Competitive paddlers should choose the option that matches their fit, weight preference, and team or coach guidance.
