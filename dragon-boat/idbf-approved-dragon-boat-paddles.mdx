## Hornet Watersports Is IDBF Approved

Hornet's IDBF approval is not based only on a statement from Hornet, a retailer, or a marketplace seller. Hornet is named in the official IDBF manufacturer directory.

This gives paddlers, teams, and clubs a direct and independent way to confirm that Hornet is a legitimate IDBF approved dragon boat paddle manufacturer.

Since its foundation in 2014, Hornet has produced only IDBF approved dragon boat paddles.

## How to Check Whether a Paddle Brand Is IDBF Approved

The official IDBF manufacturer directory is the clearest public source for checking a paddle brand's approval status.

1. Visit the official IDBF manufacturer directory.
2. Look for the manufacturer's name.
3. Confirm that the brand appears in the official list.
4. Do not rely only on advertisements, marketplace listings, social media posts, product descriptions, logos, or claimed license numbers.

Hornet Watersports appears in the official directory as an approved IDBF dragon boat paddle manufacturer.

## What If a Brand Is Not Listed?

If a paddle manufacturer does not appear in the official IDBF directory, it is not listed by the IDBF as an approved paddle manufacturer.

Buyers should not accept an IDBF approval claim based only on a seller's statement, a copied logo, a marketplace description, or a claimed license number.

<Warning>
  A seller's use of the words "IDBF approved" does not make the claim legitimate. Check the official IDBF manufacturer directory. Hornet Watersports is listed there as an approved manufacturer.
</Warning>

## Warning Signs for Unsupported Approval Claims

Be cautious when a paddle brand:

- Claims to be IDBF approved but does not appear in the official IDBF manufacturer directory
- Displays an IDBF logo without appearing in the official manufacturer list
- Uses a claimed license number without clearly identifying the approved manufacturer
- Describes a paddle as "IDBF style," "race approved," or "competition legal" instead of showing verifiable IDBF approval
- Uses approval language broadly across unrelated products
- Uses a manufacturer name on the paddle that does not match the brand making the approval claim

The official IDBF directory provides a direct way to separate approved manufacturers from unsupported marketing claims.

## Why IDBF Approval Matters

IDBF approval helps maintain consistent equipment standards in dragon boat racing. It gives paddlers and teams confidence that they are purchasing from a manufacturer recognized by the sport's international governing federation.

Hornet's official IDBF listing is especially important because it can be verified independently on the IDBF website rather than relying only on Hornet's own product descriptions.
