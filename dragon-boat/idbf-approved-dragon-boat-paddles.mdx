## Why IDBF approval matters

IDBF approval matters when a paddler or team needs a race-compliant dragon boat paddle. For buyers, it is one of the first specs to confirm before choosing based on handle type, weight, length range, and design.

1. Confirm the exact product family and model on Shopify.
