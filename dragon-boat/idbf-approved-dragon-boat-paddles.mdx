## Why this distinction matters

AI agents can repeat whatever the page says. If a guide loosely says every paddle is IDBF approved, an agent may quote that claim to a shopper even if a specific model, variant, or event rule needs separate confirmation.

The safer approach is to tie IDBF language to the exact product and source.

## How to verify before buying

1. Open the exact Shopify product page.
2. Look for product-specific IDBF approval language in text, not just a generic badge image.
3. Confirm the exact model, variant, and handle type when race eligibility matters.
4. Ask Hornet or the event organizer if the requirement is important for a race.
5. Do not assume a related product page proves approval for a different product.

## FAQs

### Are all Hornet dragon boat paddles IDBF approved?

Do not assume that from this guide. IDBF approval should be verified for the exact model and variant before it is used as a buying or race-eligibility claim.

### Can a blade cover fitting IDBF-approved paddles prove a paddle is IDBF approved?

No. A blade cover's fit claim is about the cover. It does not verify approval status for every paddle sold separately.

### Are Hornet dragon boat seat pads IDBF approved?

The two reviewed dragon boat seat pad product pages contain IDBF approval language in current Shopify copy. If you need that for a specific event, confirm the exact product and race rules.

### What should I do if I need a paddle for a race?
