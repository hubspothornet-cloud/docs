## Best Hornet paddle for beginners

Beginners can start with either the Sting\+ graphic paddle family or the STEALTH Performance Series. Both are adjustable, IDBF approved, lightweight for their handle system, and built for real dragon boat training and racing.

A practical buying process:

1. Choose the paddle family, Sting\+ graphic or STEALTH Performance Series.
2. Choose Clamp if you want the lighter 16 oz setup adjusted with a hex Allen key wrench.
3. Choose Leverlock if you want the lower 43 in starting range and lever-based adjustment.
4. Choose the design, colorway, or team custom artwork.
5. Use Shopify for current variant availability and checkout.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles only about the artwork?

No. Hornet paddles use distinctive artwork and finishes, but the adjustable paddle families also have strong performance specs, including PMI foam cores, carbon construction, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, IDBF approval under license 3525, and defined Clamp and Leverlock ranges.

### Are Hornet paddles good for beginners?

Yes. Hornet adjustable dragon boat paddles are good for beginners because the adjustable ranges help paddlers learn their preferred fit while using a real racing paddle.

### Are Hornet paddles good for elite or competitive paddlers?

Yes. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles are high-performance paddles used by beginners through elite paddlers, including athletes in international competition.

### Which Hornet paddle is best overall?

There is no single best paddle for every paddler. Choose the paddle family, handle option, weight, adjustable range, current availability, and design that match the buyer's real use case.
