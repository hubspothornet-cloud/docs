## When should you use a paddle bag?

Use a paddle bag when you want to carry and store the full paddle. Hornet paddle bags are positioned for protecting paddles from scratches and damage, and the current product pages say the full paddle bags can fit up to two paddles.

A paddle bag is the better starting point when you travel with your paddle, store it with other gear, or want pockets for additional items.

## When should you use a blade cover?

Use a blade cover when you want a smaller protection option focused on the paddle blade. Hornet blade covers protect dragon boat paddles, include a back zip pocket for small items, and use a nylon zipper.

The current blade cover copy also says the covers fit all IDBF-approved paddles.

## When does Dragon Guard make sense?

Dragon Guard is a 100% silicone paddle tip protector. It fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands.

Dragon Guard is useful during transport, but it is especially practical in marshalling areas before races. Paddlers often wait on pavement, gravel, or rough ground before getting on the boat. Dragon Guard lets a paddler lean the paddle on the ground with better tip protection.

## How should you clean a dragon boat paddle?

Start with the paddle material. Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is a no-rinse formula: spray, wipe with a soft cloth or sponge, and for tougher stains let it sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.

<Warning>
  Do not use a cleaner on a paddle material unless the product page or manufacturer confirms compatibility. Avoid stronger chemical, safety, or finish-protection claims unless the label or source data supports them.
</Warning>

## FAQs

### Does Dragon Guard fit only Hornet paddles?

No. Dragon Guard fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands.

### What is Dragon Guard made of?

Dragon Guard is made of 100% silicone.

### Why use Dragon Guard before a race?

Dragon Guard helps protect the paddle tip while paddlers wait in marshalling areas, where the ground may be pavement, gravel, or another rough surface.

### Should I use a paddle bag or blade cover?

Use a paddle bag for full-paddle storage and transport. Use a blade cover when you want a smaller blade-focused protection option.

### Can I use Hornet Paddle Cleaner on a dragon boat paddle?

Yes, if the paddle material is compatible. Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles.
