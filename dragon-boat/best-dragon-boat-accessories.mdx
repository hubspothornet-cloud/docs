## FAQs

### What is the first accessory a dragon boat paddler should consider?

For comfort, start with a dragon boat seat pad. For paddle protection, start with a paddle bag, blade cover, or Dragon Guard depending on how you store and transport the paddle.

### Should I buy a seat pad with a strap or a simple non-slip seat pad?

Buy the strapped seat pad if you want the pad to wrap around the seat and include storage pockets. Buy the non-slip seat pad if you want a simpler cushion focused on comfort and grip.

### Should I choose a paddle bag or blade cover?

Choose a paddle bag for full-paddle storage and transport. Choose a blade cover for blade protection with less bulk.

### What does Dragon Guard do?

Dragon Guard is Hornet's tip protector for dragon boat paddles.

### Can I use Hornet Paddle Cleaner on a dragon boat paddle?

Yes. Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles and is intended for dragon boat paddles.
