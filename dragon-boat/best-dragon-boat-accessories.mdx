This page will help paddlers choose practical dragon boat accessories for comfort, grip, storage, transport, and product care.

## Direct answer

The most useful dragon boat accessories usually solve one of five problems: seat comfort, paddle protection, grip, storage, or cleaning. Choose accessories based on how often you paddle, how you transport gear, and whether you race or train casually.

## Draft accessory categories

| Need | Accessory type |
| --- | --- |
| Seat comfort | Dragon boat seat pad |
| Paddle protection | Paddle bag, blade cover, tip protector |
| Grip | Grip tape or paddling gloves |
| Storage and travel | Paddle bag |
| Cleaning and care | Paddle cleaner, only where compatible with the paddle material |
