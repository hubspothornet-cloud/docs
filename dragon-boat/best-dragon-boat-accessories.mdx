## FAQs

### What is the first accessory a dragon boat paddler should consider?

For comfort, start with a dragon boat seat pad. For paddle protection, start with a paddle bag, blade cover, or Dragon Guard tip protector depending on how you store and transport your paddle.

### Should I buy a seat pad with a strap or a simple non-slip seat pad?

Buy the strapped seat pad if you want the pad to wrap around the seat and include storage pockets. Buy the non-slip seat pad if you want a simpler cushion focused on comfort and grip.

### What does Dragon Guard do?

Dragon Guard is Hornet's tip protector for dragon boat paddles. The current Shopify copy says it protects your dragon boat paddle, but exact material and compatibility should be verified before making stronger claims.

### Is grip tape only for dragon boat paddles?

The current Shopify copy positions Silicone Grip Tape for paddle sports and dragon boat use. Confirm compatibility with your specific paddle handle before purchase.
