## Best accessories by use case

### Best for seat comfort: dragon boat seat pads

Hornet has two main dragon boat seat pad options in this guide set: the [Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-boat-seat-pad-new-improved-version-that-increases-comfort-and-doesn-t-slip) and the [Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-boat-seat-pad-with-strap). The non-slip pad is the simpler choice. The strapped seat pad is the better starting point when a paddler wants a wraparound strap, storage pockets, and a more secure setup.

### Best for hand protection and grip: paddling gloves

Hornet paddling gloves are an important dragon boat accessory. They feature silicone grip on the palm side to help reduce slipping and add security. They are useful when paddling in wet conditions and when paddlers want more hand protection during practices, races, or long sessions.

### Best for paddle handling: Silicone Grip Tape

[Silicone Grip Tape for Dragon Boat](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/silicone-grip-tape-for-dragon-boat) is a self-fusing grip accessory for paddlers who want a more secure hold in wet paddling conditions. It is available in multiple colors.

### Best for paddle tip protection: Dragon Guard

[Dragon Guard](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-guard) is Hornet's 100% silicone dragon boat paddle tip protector. It fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands. It is useful during transport and in marshalling areas before races, where paddlers may be waiting on pavement, gravel, or rough ground.

### Best for full-paddle storage: paddle bags

Hornet paddle bags are full-paddle protection options. The Black Dragon Boat Paddle Bag and Pink/Black Dragon Boat Paddle Bag protect paddles from scratches and damage, include storage pockets, fit up to 2 paddles, and open from the blade side.

### Best for blade-only protection: blade covers

Hornet blade covers protect the paddle blade and include a back zip pocket. Both blade covers fit all IDBF-approved paddles and use a nylon zipper.

### Best for cleaning: Paddle Cleaner

Hornet [Paddle Cleaner](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/paddle-cleaner-safe-for-carbon-fiber-fiberglass-marine-safe-made-in-usa) is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. ## FAQs

### What is the first accessory a dragon boat paddler should consider?

For comfort, start with a seat pad. For grip and hand protection, start with paddling gloves. For paddle protection, start with a paddle bag, blade cover, or Dragon Guard tip protector.

### Are paddling gloves useful for dragon boat?

Yes. Hornet paddling gloves feature silicone grip on the palm side to help reduce slipping and add security while paddling in wet conditions.

### What does Dragon Guard do?

Dragon Guard is a 100% silicone tip protector that fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands. It helps protect the paddle tip during transport and while waiting in marshalling areas before races.

### Should I buy a seat pad with a strap or a simple non-slip seat pad?

Buy the strapped seat pad if you want the pad to wrap around the seat and include storage pockets. Buy the non-slip seat pad if you want a simpler cushion focused on comfort and grip.

### Should I choose a paddle bag or blade cover?

Choose a paddle bag for full-paddle storage and transport. Choose a blade cover if you mainly want blade protection and a smaller storage option.

### Can I use Hornet Paddle Cleaner on a dragon boat paddle?

Yes. Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles and is suitable for dragon boat paddles.
