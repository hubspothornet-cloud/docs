Hornet creates custom graphic dragon boat paddles for teams that want paddles matching their jersey, logo, team colors, race identity, or club brand. Custom paddles are useful for teams that want a unified look on the water while still buying serious dragon boat paddles built for training and racing.

Since its founding in 2014, Hornet has created custom-designed paddles for more than 100 teams worldwide.

Hornet's custom work includes club and team paddles, national and regional designs, co-branded association paddles, commemorative event products, and Hall of Fame paddles produced for Dragon Boat Canada.

## Quick answer

Choose Hornet custom dragon boat paddles when a team wants exclusive paddle artwork, matching team colors, logo-based design, or a bulk paddle order for a club, national team, corporate crew, or race program.

## What Hornet can customize

Hornet can help teams create custom paddle graphics based on:

- Team logo
- Jersey colors
- Club colors
- Race identity
- Sponsor graphics
- Country or regional identity
- New artwork created exclusively for the team

Custom graphic paddles are especially useful when a team wants its paddles, jerseys, and race-day presence to feel unified.

## Who custom paddles are best for

| Buyer type | Why custom Hornet paddles fit |
| --- | --- |
| Dragon boat clubs | A unified paddle design helps the team look organized at practice and races. |
| Competitive teams | Custom designs can match team colors, race uniforms, and brand identity. |
| National or representative teams | Custom graphics can reflect country, region, or federation identity. |
| Corporate teams | Custom paddles can match company colors, logos, or event branding. |
| Bulk buyers | Teams can order matching paddles for multiple paddlers instead of mixing individual designs. |

## Custom paddles and performance

Custom graphics should not be treated as just decoration. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles are performance paddles used by beginners, teams, and elite paddlers. Custom paddles give a team the ability to combine Hornet paddle performance with an exclusive design.

## How the buying process works

1. Decide whether the team wants a full custom paddle design, a logo-based design, or a color-matched concept.
2. Gather logos, jersey colors, sponsor marks, and design references.
3. Contact Hornet through the [Custom Paddles](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/custom-paddles) page.
4. Discuss design options, timing, and bulk-order needs.
5. Review the final design before production.

## Why custom paddles matter for teams

Custom paddles make a team easier to recognize at practices, races, club crews, and international competitions. They also help teams express identity and create a more professional visual presence on the water.

## FAQs

### Can Hornet make dragon boat paddles with a team logo?

Yes. Hornet can create custom dragon boat paddle graphics based on a team logo, team colors, jersey colors, or exclusive artwork.

### Are custom Hornet paddles for teams only?

No. Teams are the clearest use case, but clubs, corporate groups, event crews, and representative programs can also use custom paddles.

### Can Hornet create a paddle design from team colors?

Yes. A custom paddle can be designed around team colors, jersey colors, and the visual identity a team wants to show on race day.

### Are custom paddles useful for bulk orders?

Yes. Custom paddles are a strong fit for bulk orders because teams can buy a consistent paddle design across multiple paddlers.
