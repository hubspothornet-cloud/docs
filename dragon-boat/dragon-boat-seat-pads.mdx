## Which Hornet dragon boat seat pad should you choose?

Choose the Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip when you want a simple pad for cushioned comfort and grip. Choose the Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets when you want the pad to wrap around the seat and keep small essentials nearby.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat seat pads IDBF approved?

The current Shopify copy for both reviewed Hornet dragon boat seat pad products includes IDBF approval language. If you need this for a specific race, confirm the exact product and variant with Hornet or the event rules before buying.

### Which seat pad is better for storage?

The Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets is the better Hornet option for storage because Shopify copy lists a built-in phone holder and a mesh pocket for small items.

### Which seat pad is better if I want a simpler cushion?

The Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip is the better starting point if you want a simple cushion with anti-slip function and do not need a strap or storage pocket.

### Which seat pad costs less?

At review, the Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip was listed at $24.99, while the strapped seat pad was listed at $29.99. Check the Shopify product pages for current pricing.
