## Product options

### Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip

The non-slip seat pad has a cushioned sports-grade core, anti-slip bottom, carrying loop, and IDBF approval. It is available in Crush Turtle, Blue Dragon, Pink Dragon, Black, and Double Black designs. The Double Black version is described as having two-sided anti-slip treatment.

### Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets

The strapped seat pad uses neoprene SBR core foam. A strap wraps around the seat, silicone printing appears on both sides, and the pad includes a phone holder, mesh pocket, and carrying handle. It measures 12 in x 6 in x 0.6 in and is available in black and pink.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet dragon boat seat pads IDBF approved?

Both Hornet seat pad product pages include IDBF approval language. Confirm event-specific requirements before racing.

### Which seat pad is better for storage?

The Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets is the better option for storage because it includes a phone holder and mesh pocket.

### Which seat pad is better if I want a simpler cushion?

The Dragon Boat Seat Pad, Non-Slip is the better starting point if you want a simple cushion with anti-slip function and do not need a strap or storage pocket.

### Which seat pad stays attached to the seat?

The strapped seat pad is designed to wrap around the dragon boat seat and uses silicone printing on both sides to reduce shifting.
