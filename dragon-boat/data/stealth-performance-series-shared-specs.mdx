The STEALTH Performance Series is Hornet's next evolution in dragon boat paddle design, built for athletes who want elite performance with a modern visual edge.

## Safe guide language

- STEALTH paddles are IDBF approved under license 3525.
- STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction, so no two paddles are exactly alike.
- STEALTH paddles are engineered for high-level racing with stiffness, responsiveness, and durability as core performance goals.
- The lightweight PMI foam core and reinforced Diamond Edge blade are designed to support powerful, efficient strokes while helping prevent chips and cracks over time.
- The glossy finish and metallic accents are part of the STEALTH visual identity.
- The Clamp Adjustable option weighs approximately 16 oz and adjusts from 45 in to 51 in.
- The Leverlock Adjustable option weighs approximately 20 oz and adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.

## Buyer positioning

Use STEALTH paddles when buyers want Hornet's elite racing-focused paddle family, modern marbleized carbon construction, a glossy performance finish, and adjustable handle options. The final choice still depends on handle preference, fit, current inventory, and preferred colorway.
