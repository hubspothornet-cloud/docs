## Product summary

Dragon Guard is Hornet's 100% silicone dragon boat paddle tip protector. It fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands.

## Safe facts

- Product type: dragon boat paddle tip protector.
- Material: 100% silicone.
- Compatibility: fits IDBF-approved paddles from Hornet and other brands.
- Use case: protects the paddle tip during transport.
- Race-day use case: useful in marshalling areas before races, where paddlers may wait on pavement, gravel, or rough ground and lean the paddle on the ground.
- Public product URL: [Dragon Guard](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-guard).

## Copy guidance

Use Dragon Guard as a practical paddle-protection accessory, especially for travel and race-day staging before getting on the boat. It should not be described as only fitting Hornet paddles.
