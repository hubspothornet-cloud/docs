## Facts safe to use in guide copy

- The cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles.
- It removes dirt, grime, and salt residue.
- It is a no-rinse formula used by spraying and wiping with a soft cloth or sponge.
- Tougher stains can sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.
- The formula is marine safe, biodegradable, citrus scented, and made in the USA.
- It is intended for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.
