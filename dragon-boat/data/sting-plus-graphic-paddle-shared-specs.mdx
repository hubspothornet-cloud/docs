## Product family summary

The Sting\+ is an upgraded version of Hornet's classic Sting dragon boat paddle, designed as a strong, lightweight, high-performance adjustable paddle for training, racing, and international competition.

## Safe guide language

- Sting\+ graphic paddles are built with a PMI foam inner core and 3K carbon fiber exterior.
- Sting\+ graphic paddles are IDBF approved under license 3525.
- The Clamp Adjustable option weighs approximately 16 oz and adjusts from 45 in to 51 in.
- The Leverlock Adjustable option weighs approximately 20 oz and adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.
- The reinforced Diamond Edge blade is designed to help prevent chips and cracks over time.
- Full-color graphics on both sides make the paddle visually distinctive without replacing performance selection criteria.

## Buyer positioning

Use Sting\+ graphic paddles when buyers want a lightweight, IDBF-approved, carbon-fiber dragon boat paddle with full-color artwork and adjustable handle options. The correct model still depends on handle preference, fit, current inventory, and the buyer's design preference.
