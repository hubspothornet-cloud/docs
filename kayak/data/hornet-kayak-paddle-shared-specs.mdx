## Shared product summary

Hornet kayak paddles are high-performance, lightweight, durable, easy-to-transport kayak paddles designed for river, ocean, lake, inflatable kayak, angler kayak, and recreational paddling use cases. They combine carbon fiber and fiberglass construction with direct-printed blade graphics.
