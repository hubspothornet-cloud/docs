## Step-by-step selection process

### 1. Confirm paddle length first

Paddle length is one of the most important fit details, but the reviewed product text needs clearer length data. Confirm length on the product page or product image before buying.

### 2. Use weight as a comfort clue

Current Shopify copy for the reviewed Hornet kayak paddles says they weigh 26 oz. A lighter paddle can be easier to use during longer paddling sessions, but weight alone is not the whole decision.

### 3. Confirm material and construction

The current pages include useful images and marketing copy, but the guide should not make final construction claims until shaft material, blade material, number of pieces, and adjustability are verified in text.

### 4. Pick the design last

Hornet kayak paddles use distinctive artwork. Choose the design you like after verifying the paddle's fit, weight, and construction match your use case.

## What to verify before buying

- Paddle length.
- Shaft and blade material.
- Whether the paddle is adjustable.
- Adjustment range if adjustable.
- Number of pieces for travel or storage.
- Feathering options if applicable.
- Current price and inventory.
