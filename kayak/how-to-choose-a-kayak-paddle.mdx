## Step-by-step selection process

### 1. Confirm the shared platform fits your use case

Hornet positions the paddle for rivers, oceans, lakes, inflatable kayaks, angler kayaks, and other recreational vessels. The carbon fiber and fiberglass construction is intended to balance durability with useful flex.

### 2. Confirm length and feathering preferences

The shaft adjusts from 90.5 in to 94.5 in. Blade rotation runs from 0 to 60 degrees, and feathering can be set in 15-degree increments.

### 3. Consider storage and transport

The paddle breaks down into 2 pieces and includes 2 drip rings. This is useful for vehicle transport, home storage, and travel.

### 4. Choose the graphic

Because all Hornet kayak paddles share the same platform, choose Bass Attack for a fishing theme, Paw Print for dog lovers, Making Waves for a water-inspired design, Blue or Green Scales for bold color, and Crush for recognizable Hornet artwork.

### What are Hornet kayak paddles made of?
