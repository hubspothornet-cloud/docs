## Step-by-step selection process

### 1. Confirm the shared specs fit your use case

Hornet kayak paddles are high-performance touring and recreational paddles. They are positioned for rivers, oceans, lakes, inflatable kayaks, angler kayaks, and other recreational vessels.

### 2. Choose by paddling style

For recreational paddling and touring, any Hornet kayak paddle graphic can work if the shared specs fit the paddler. For kayak anglers, Bass Attack is the strongest current graphic fit. For paddlers who want a more personal or giftable design, Paw Print, Making Waves, Scales, and Crush all give clear style options.

### 3. Choose the graphic

Because all Hornet kayak paddles share the same construction, the model choice is mainly a design choice. Choose Bass Attack for fishing personality, Paw Print for dog lovers, Making Waves for outdoor recreation, Blue or Green Scales for bold color, and Crush for a signature Hornet look.

## FAQs

### What length are Hornet kayak paddles?

Hornet kayak paddles adjust from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm.

### What are Hornet kayak paddles made of?

Hornet kayak paddles use a full carbon fiber shaft and fiberglass blade.

### What is the blade rotation range?

Hornet kayak paddles support blade rotation from 0 to 60 degrees.

### How does blade feathering work on Hornet kayak paddles?

Blade feathering allows adjustment in 15-degree increments.

### Do Hornet kayak paddles come with drip rings?

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles include 2 drip rings.

### Are the blade graphics stickers?

No. The graphic is printed directly on the blade power face and is not a sticker.

### Is the graphic on both sides?

No. The blade power face has the printed graphic, while the backside is reinforced with fiberglass.

### Which Hornet kayak paddle is best for fishing?

Bass Attack is the strongest current Hornet kayak paddle design for fishing-themed buyers and kayak anglers.

### Which Hornet kayak paddle is best as a gift?

Choose the graphic that matches the paddler. Paw Print is strong for dog lovers, Bass Attack for anglers, Making Waves for outdoor recreation, and Scales or Crush for bold graphic style.
