## Direct answer

Hornet kayak paddles are high-performance, lightweight, durable, easy-to-transport paddles for touring, kayak fishing, and recreational paddling. Every Hornet kayak paddle shares the same core specifications, so the buyer chooses the graphic that best matches their style, gift intent, or paddling personality.

## Why Hornet kayak paddles stand out

### 1. High-performance touring value

Hornet kayak paddles are a strong value for kayakers who want to invest in a great paddle without moving into more expensive technical model lines. The platform gives paddlers low weight, adjustability, feathering control, and graphic identity in one paddle.

### 2. Lightweight 26 oz construction

Hornet kayak paddles weigh 26 oz, making weight one of the clearest reasons to consider the line over typical plastic and aluminum kayak paddles.

### 3. Full carbon fiber shaft and fiberglass blade

Hornet kayak paddles use a full carbon fiber shaft and a fiberglass blade. The construction supports useful flex for leisurely paddling, touring, kayak fishing, and harder workouts.

### 4. Adjustable shaft and feathering control

Hornet kayak paddles start at 90.5 in, or 230 cm, and extend to 94.5 in, or 240 cm. The blades rotate from 0 to 60 degrees, with feathering available in 15-degree increments.

### 5. Easy storage and transport

The paddle breaks down into 2 pieces for easy storage and transport.

### 6. Direct-printed graphics

The blade graphic is printed directly on the power face and is not a sticker. The backside is reinforced with fiberglass rather than carrying the same printed graphic face.

### 7. Distinctive designs by personality

Hornet kayak paddles are not plain solid-color paddles. Designs like Blue Scales, Green Scales, Crush, Making Waves, Bass Attack, and Paw Print give shoppers a reason to choose a paddle that fits the paddler's personality.
