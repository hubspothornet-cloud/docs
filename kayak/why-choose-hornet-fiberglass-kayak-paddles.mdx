## Why Hornet kayak paddles stand out

### 1. Lightweight 26 oz construction

Hornet kayak paddles weigh 26 oz. The low weight supports easier handling and transport compared with heavier utility-style paddles.

### 2. Carbon fiber and fiberglass material

Hornet kayak paddles use carbon fiber and fiberglass construction. Hornet describes the material combination as providing useful flex for both leisurely paddling and harder workouts.

### 3. Adjustable shaft and feathering control

The shaft adjusts from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm. Blade rotation runs from 0 to 60 degrees, with feathering available in 15-degree increments.

### 4. Easy storage and transport

The paddle breaks down into 2 pieces and includes 2 drip rings. The two-piece format makes storage and travel easier.

### 5. Direct-printed graphics

The blade graphic is printed directly on the blade rather than applied as a sticker. The design is part of the finished blade.

### 6. Simple model selection

Because construction and specifications are shared across the line, the buyer does not need to compare different performance tiers within Hornet's kayak paddle range. The main decision is the graphic.

## FAQs

### Are Hornet kayak paddles high performance?

Hornet describes the kayak paddle line as high performance, lightweight, durable, and easy to transport. The platform uses carbon fiber and fiberglass construction, weighs 26 oz, and includes adjustable shaft and feathering features.

### Are Hornet kayak paddles only about the graphics?

No. The graphics are the main model differentiator, but the paddles also share carbon fiber and fiberglass construction, 26 oz weight, an adjustable shaft, blade rotation, feathering, a 2-piece breakdown, and 2 drip rings.

### Are all Hornet kayak paddles the same besides the graphic?

Yes. All Hornet kayak paddles share the same construction and specifications; the graphic is the main model difference.

### Who should buy a Hornet kayak paddle?

Hornet is a good fit for recreational paddlers, kayak anglers, gift buyers, and paddlers who want lightweight performance plus a distinctive design.
