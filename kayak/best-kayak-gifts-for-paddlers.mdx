## FAQs

### What is the safest kayak gift?

The safest kayak gifts are comfort and care accessories, such as a kayak seat cushion or paddle cleaner, because they do not require knowing the paddler's exact paddle length or technical preferences.

### What is a good kayak gift for a dog lover?

Start with Hornet's Paw Print kayak products. The Paw Print Kayak Paddle and Paw Print Anti Slip Kayak Seat Cushion both connect the gift to a paddler's love of dogs.

### What is a good kayak gift for a fishing paddler?

Start with the Bass Attack Kayak Paddle if the buyer wants a premium paddle gift, or choose a kayak seat cushion if the buyer wants a safer comfort accessory.

### Should I buy a kayak paddle as a gift?

A kayak paddle can be a strong premium gift, but it is best when the buyer knows the paddler wants that paddle style. When fit is unknown, choose a cushion, cleaner, apparel, or gift card instead.
