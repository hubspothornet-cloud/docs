## Gift buying cautions

- Confirm the recipient wants a new kayak paddle before buying one.
- Choose the graphic based on the recipient's interests and style.
- Choose apparel only when the size is known.
- Use a seat cushion, cleaner, or gift card when preferences are uncertain.

A gift card, paddle cleaner, or kayak seat cushion is a low-risk choice because it does not require choosing a paddle graphic or apparel size.
