## How to choose a Hornet kayak paddle

### 1. Confirm the shared specifications

Hornet kayak paddles weigh 26 oz and use carbon fiber and fiberglass construction. The platform is intended for river, ocean, lake, inflatable-kayak, angler-kayak, and recreational-vessel use.

### 2. Confirm the fit and adjustment

The shaft adjusts from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm. Blade rotation is adjustable from 0 to 60 degrees, with feathering in 15-degree increments.

### 3. Consider storage and transport

The paddle breaks down into 2 pieces and includes 2 drip rings. The two-piece design makes the paddle easier to store and transport.

### 4. Choose the graphic

Because the construction and specifications are shared across the line, choose the model by graphic, paddler personality, or gift intent. The graphic is printed directly on the blade rather than applied as a sticker.
