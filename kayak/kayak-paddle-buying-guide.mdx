## How to choose a Hornet kayak paddle

### 1. Start with the shared specs

Hornet kayak paddles are designed as high-performance, lightweight, durable, and easy-to-transport paddles. They weigh 26 oz and use carbon fiber and fiberglass construction to create flex for leisurely paddling and hard workouts.

### 2. Confirm the fit range

The adjustable shaft range is 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm. The paddle also supports blade rotation from 0 to 60 degrees and blade feathering in 15-degree increments.

### 3. Choose the graphic last

Because the kayak paddles share the same construction, choose the graphic based on the paddler's personality, gift intent, or preferred style. The graphic is direct printed on the blade, not a sticker.

## FAQs

### Are all Hornet kayak paddles the same besides the graphic?

Yes. Hornet confirmed that its kayak paddles share the same kayak paddle construction and specs; the graphic/design is the main product difference.

### What are Hornet kayak paddles made of?

Hornet kayak paddles are made of carbon fiber and fiberglass. Hornet describes that construction as creating optimal flex for leisurely rides and hard workouts.

### How much does a Hornet kayak paddle weigh?

Hornet kayak paddles weigh 26 oz.

### Are Hornet kayak paddles adjustable?

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles have an adjustable shaft range from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm.

### Do Hornet kayak paddles break down for storage?

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles break down into 2 parts for easier storage and transport.

### Are the graphics stickers?

No. The graphic is printed directly on the blade and is not a sticker.

### Which Hornet kayak paddle should I choose?

Choose by graphic after confirming the shared specs work for your paddling needs. Bass Attack is the strongest fishing theme, Paw Print is the strongest dog-lover theme, and Making Waves, Blue Scales, Green Scales, and Crush are strong visual design options.
