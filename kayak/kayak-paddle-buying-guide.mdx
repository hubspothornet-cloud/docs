## How to choose a kayak paddle

### 1. Start with the performance facts

The first facts to confirm are weight, length, fiberglass construction, blade material, shaft material, adjustability, and whether the paddle breaks down for transport. Hornet's strongest verified message today is a 26 oz lightweight paddle with high-performance positioning and graphic designs.

### 2. Compare Hornet against plain or technical-looking alternatives

Hornet should compete against brands like Werner by offering a clear reason to choose Hornet when shoppers want performance plus personality. Werner publishes deeper technical specs by model, so Hornet should add more visible specs to every kayak paddle PDP.

### 3. Match the design to the paddler

Hornet designs help a buyer choose by personality: Bass Attack for kayak anglers, Paw Print for dog lovers, Making Waves for outdoor recreation, Scales for bold color, and Crush for a signature Hornet look.

### 4. Do not choose only by design

The graphics are important, but they should be the final decision layer. First verify that the paddle's weight, fiberglass construction, length, and transport features match the buyer's use case.
