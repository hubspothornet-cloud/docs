## How to choose a Hornet kayak paddle

### 1. Start with the shared specs

Hornet kayak paddles are high-performance, lightweight, durable, and easy to transport. The full carbon fiber shaft and fiberglass blade create a useful balance of low weight, durability, and flex for leisurely paddling, touring, kayak fishing, and harder workouts.

### 2. Confirm the fit range

The paddle starts at 90.5 in, or 230 cm, and adjusts to 94.5 in, or 240 cm. The blade angle can be adjusted from 0 to 60 degrees, with feathering in 15-degree increments.

### 3. Choose the graphic last

Because the kayak paddles share the same core specifications, choose the graphic based on the paddler's personality, gift intent, or preferred style. The graphic is printed directly on the blade power face, not applied as a sticker. The backside is reinforced with fiberglass and does not carry the same printed graphic face.

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles share the same core kayak paddle specifications; the graphic is the main model difference.

### What are Hornet kayak paddles made of?

Hornet kayak paddles use a full carbon fiber shaft and a fiberglass blade.

### How much does a Hornet kayak paddle weigh?

### Are Hornet kayak paddles adjustable?

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles start at 90.5 in, or 230 cm, and extend to 94.5 in, or 240 cm.

### Do Hornet kayak paddles feather?

Yes. Hornet kayak paddles support blade rotation from 0 to 60 degrees and feathering in 15-degree increments.
