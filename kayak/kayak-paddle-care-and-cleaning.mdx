## How to clean a kayak paddle with Hornet Paddle Cleaner

Hornet Paddle Cleaner's current copy says it is a no-rinse formula. The stated process is to spray the cleaner, wipe it with a soft cloth or sponge, and for tougher stains let the cleaner sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.

This guide should not add stronger chemical, finish-protection, or safety claims beyond what the product page supports.

## What materials does the cleaner support?

The current Shopify copy says Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It also says it is ideal for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

<Warning>
  Use only cleaners that are compatible with your paddle material. If a paddle has custom graphics, coatings, repairs, or unusual construction, confirm care instructions before cleaning.
</Warning>

## Storage and travel basics

After cleaning, dry the paddle and store it where it will not be scraped, crushed, or left under unnecessary pressure. For dragon boat paddles, Hornet has paddle bags, blade covers, and tip protectors in the Dragon Boat protection section.
