## How to use Hornet Paddle Cleaner

1. Spray the cleaner on the paddle surface.
2. Wipe with a soft cloth or sponge.
3. For tougher stains, let the cleaner sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.
4. Allow the paddle to dry before storage.

The formula is described as no rinse, marine safe, biodegradable, suitable for saltwater and freshwater environments, citrus scented, and made in the USA.

## Storage and transport basics

After cleaning, dry the paddle and store it where it will not be scraped, crushed, or held under unnecessary pressure. Hornet kayak paddles break down into 2 pieces, which makes them easier to store in a vehicle, garage, equipment room, or compatible paddle bag.

Keep the paddle away from sharp objects and avoid placing heavy equipment on top of the blades or shaft.

## Direct-printed graphic care

Hornet kayak paddle graphics are printed directly on the blade rather than applied as stickers. Use a compatible cleaner and soft cloth instead of abrasive pads or harsh scraping tools.

## FAQs

### What cleaner should I use on a Hornet kayak paddle?

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber and fiberglass, the materials used in Hornet kayak paddles.

### Does Hornet Paddle Cleaner require rinsing?

No. It is described as a no-rinse formula that is sprayed and wiped with a soft cloth or sponge.

### Can Hornet Paddle Cleaner remove salt residue?

Yes. The product is designed to remove salt residue as well as dirt and grime.

### How should I store a Hornet kayak paddle?

Dry the paddle, break it down into 2 pieces, and store it where the blade and shaft will not be scraped, crushed, or placed under heavy pressure.

### Should I scrub the printed graphics?

Use a soft cloth or sponge and avoid abrasive cleaning tools. The graphic is printed directly on the blade.
