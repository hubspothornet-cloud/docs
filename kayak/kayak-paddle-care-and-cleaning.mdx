## How to clean a Hornet kayak paddle

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is a no-rinse formula. Spray the cleaner, wipe it with a soft cloth or sponge, and for tougher stains let the cleaner sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.

This guide should not add stronger chemical, finish-protection, or safety claims beyond what the product page supports.

## What materials does the cleaner support?

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is also suitable for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

That makes it a natural match for Hornet kayak paddles, which use full carbon fiber shafts and fiberglass blades.

## Caring for direct-printed graphics

Hornet kayak paddle graphics are printed directly on the blade power face and are not stickers. The backside of the blade is reinforced fiberglass. Clean both sides with product-compatible care methods and avoid abrasive handling that could damage the paddle finish.

## Storage and travel basics

After cleaning, dry the paddle and store it where it will not be scraped, crushed, or left under unnecessary pressure. Hornet kayak paddles break down into 2 pieces, which makes storage and transport easier.

<Warning>
  Use only cleaners that are compatible with your paddle material. If a paddle has custom graphics, coatings, repairs, or unusual construction, confirm care instructions before cleaning.
</Warning>
