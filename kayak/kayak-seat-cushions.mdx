## Why a kayak seat cushion matters

Kayak seats can become uncomfortable during long paddling sessions, fishing trips, recreational outings, or canoe use. A cushion is one of the easiest upgrades because it improves seat comfort without changing the kayak, paddle, or boat setup.

## How to choose a kayak seat cushion

### 1. Start with comfort

Use a kayak seat cushion when your goal is simple comfort during paddling. The Anti Slip Kayak Seat Cushion is the main Hornet product to start with for kayaking, canoeing, fishing, and other small-vessel use.

### 2. Choose the design

Choose the standard Anti Slip Kayak Seat Cushion when you want the simplest and most proven Hornet option. Choose Paw Print when the buyer wants a dog-themed gift or a matching Paw Print design. Choose Crush when that graphic is available and the buyer wants the signature Hornet look.

### 3. Confirm the fit and dimensions

Check the current product page for dimensions, thickness, materials, and seat fit. Kayak and canoe seats vary, so measure the available space before buying when fit is critical.

### 4. Keep comfort claims practical

The cushion is a comfort accessory. It should not be described as treating, preventing, or curing back pain, injury, numbness, or medical conditions.

- Confirm exact cushion dimensions.
- Confirm material and foam composition.

- Confirm current price and inventory.
