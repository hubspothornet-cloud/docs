## How to choose a kayak seat cushion

### 1. Start with the use case

Use a kayak seat cushion when the goal is added comfort during paddling. Hornet positions the standard cushion for kayaks, canoes, fishing vessels, and other small vessels.

### 2. Choose the design

Choose the standard version for the simplest look. Choose Paw Print when the paddler wants a dog-themed design or a gift that matches the Paw Print kayak paddle.

### 3. Confirm the fit

Check the current product page for dimensions, thickness, materials, and seat fit. Kayak and canoe seats vary, so measure the available space before buying when fit is critical.

### 4. Keep comfort expectations practical

A cushion may improve comfort, grip, and the feel of the seat. It should not be treated as a medical product or a guaranteed solution for pain, numbness, or injury.

## What to confirm before buying

- Exact cushion dimensions
- Material and foam composition
- Thickness
- Anti-slip surface placement
- Fit with the exact kayak, canoe, or small-vessel seat
- Current price, inventory, shipping, and warranty details
