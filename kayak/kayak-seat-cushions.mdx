This page will explain how to compare kayak seat cushions and comfort accessories.

## Direct answer

A kayak seat cushion should improve comfort without making the paddler feel unstable in the boat. Choose based on seat fit, cushion thickness, non-slip features, drainage, and how long you usually paddle.
