Hornet Watersports Kayak Guides help paddlers compare kayak paddles, seat cushions, comfort accessories, and paddle care products. This section is for recreational paddlers, kayak anglers, and casual paddlers who want clearer guidance before choosing gear.

<Note>
  This section is intentionally smaller than Dragon Boat. It should stay focused on real Hornet products and verified product facts instead of trying to look like a full kayak encyclopedia.
</Note>

## What this section will cover

- How to choose a kayak paddle
- Paddle length, comfort, and use-case considerations
- Kayak seat cushions and paddling comfort
- Paddle care and cleaning
- Product comparisons where Hornet has enough verified product data

## How to choose what to read first

| Situation | Start with |
| --- | --- |
| You need a general buying overview | Kayak Paddle Buying Guide |
| You are choosing paddle size or style | How to Choose a Kayak Paddle |
| You want more seat comfort | Kayak Seat Cushions |
| You want to clean or protect a paddle | Kayak Paddle Care and Cleaning |

## Source-of-truth rule

These guides should explain how to choose the right type of product. The Shopify product page remains the source of truth for pricing, availability, color options, product specs, and checkout details.
