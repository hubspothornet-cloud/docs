## Hornet kayak paddle specifications

- Carbon fiber and fiberglass construction
- 26 oz weight
- Adjustable shaft from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm
- Blade rotation from 0 to 60 degrees
- Feathering in 15-degree increments
- 2-piece breakdown for storage and transport
- 2 drip rings
- Direct-printed blade graphics, not stickers
- Shared specifications across the line, with graphics differing by model
