The AEO Growth Loop is designed to compound over time, so success looks different in week 1 vs. week 8. These three metrics tell the full story — and they require data from different places.

## The Three Metrics

  <Card title="Agentic Storefronts Panel" icon="chart-line">

  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Referrer Trend" icon="arrow-trend-up">

  <Card title="New-Customer Share" icon="user-plus">

## Metric 1: Agentic Storefronts Panel

**Where to find it:** Shopify Admin → Agentic Storefronts

**What it shows:** AI-agent sessions, revenue attributed to AI agents, and which products are being cited and converted.

**How to use it:** Check weekly. Track the trend for each of your four core products after the loop has made a deep pass on them.

<Warning>
  This panel is not exposed via the Shopify API or ShopifyQL. The loop cannot read or report from it — you have to check it manually.
</Warning>

## Metric 2: AI Referrer Trend

The loop's daily run report includes AI-referrer traffic data for the focus product — sessions from chatgpt.com, bing.com/chat, perplexity.ai, and similar sources.

Treat the absolute number as a lower bound — most agentic sessions don't pass referrer headers. Track the *growth rate* of this number week-over-week. A consistent upward trend indicates improving Share of Model.

<Note>
  A product that goes from 3 AI-referrer sessions/week to 12 AI-referrer sessions/week has shown strong Share of Model growth, even if the absolute numbers seem small.
</Note>

## Metric 3: New-Customer Share

AI-agent recommendations are a top-of-funnel discovery channel. When the loop improves a product's Share of Model, you should see its new-customer purchase rate increase.

Track this per product in your Shopify analytics: filter orders for the focus product and look at first-time buyer percentage. Compare pre- and post-loop-deep-pass results to measure the impact.

## Suggested Review Cadence

| Check | Frequency | Where |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Run report | Daily | Claude session output |
| AI referrer trends | Daily (in report) | Loop run report |
| Agentic Storefronts | Weekly | Shopify Admin |
| New-customer share | Monthly | Shopify Analytics |
