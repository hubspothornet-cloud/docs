This glossary defines common Hornet Watersports product terms used across the guide center. Product-specific claims should still be checked against the relevant product fact page and Shopify product page.

## Dragon boat paddle terms

### Sting\+

Sting\+ is Hornet's upgraded adjustable dragon boat paddle platform. The verified graphic Sting\+ paddles use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, 3K twill carbon fiber weave, matte finish, full-color graphics on both sides, Diamond Edge blade reinforcement, removable non-slip grip covers, and IDBF license 3525.

### STEALTH Performance Series

STEALTH Performance Series is Hornet's elite racing-focused dragon boat paddle family. STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction, high-performance PMI foam cores, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, glossy performance finishes with metallic accents, removable non-slip grip covers, and IDBF license 3525.

### Clamp Adjustable

Clamp Adjustable is one of Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle handle options. Hornet's verified shared specs list Clamp Adjustable at approximately 16 oz with a 45 in to 51 in adjustable range.

### Leverlock Adjustable

Leverlock Adjustable is one of Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle handle options. Hornet's verified shared specs list Leverlock Adjustable at approximately 20 oz with a 43 in to 51 in adjustable range.

### PMI foam core

PMI foam core refers to the inner core material used in Hornet's verified Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddle specs.

### 3K carbon fiber

3K carbon fiber is the exterior construction listed for Hornet's verified Sting\+ graphic paddles. The graphic Sting\+ shared specs also mention 3K twill carbon fiber weave.

### Marbleized carbon construction

Marbleized carbon construction is the distinctive STEALTH Performance Series construction look. Hornet's STEALTH spec note says no two paddles are exactly alike.

### Diamond Edge

Diamond Edge is Hornet's proprietary reinforced blade edge used on verified Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles. Hornet describes it as helping prevent chips and cracks over time.

### IDBF license 3525

Hornet's reviewed Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series adjustable dragon boat paddle families are IDBF approved under license 3525. Buyers should still check event-specific rules before racing.

## Accessory terms

### Paddle blade cover

A paddle blade cover is a blade-focused protection accessory. Hornet blade cover product pages list back zip pockets and fit for IDBF approved paddles.

### Paddle bag

A paddle bag is a full-paddle storage or transport accessory. Current Hornet full paddle bag copy says the bags protect paddles from scratches and damage and fit up to two paddles.

### Dragon Guard

Dragon Guard is Hornet's dragon boat paddle tip protector.

### Silicone grip tape

Silicone grip tape is a self-fusing grip accessory used to help paddlers maintain a more secure hold in wet paddle sport conditions.

### Paddle cleaner

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. Current Shopify copy says it is ideal for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

## Rowing and kayak terms

### Concept2 compatibility

Use Concept2 compatibility only where the product title or product copy supports it. Current Hornet rowing machine cushion pages include several Concept2-focused products, but exact dimensions should be checked on Shopify.

### Kayak paddle graphics

Hornet kayak paddles use bold graphic designs. Guide pages should still lead with verified weight, fiberglass performance positioning, construction, use case, and fit before design preference.
