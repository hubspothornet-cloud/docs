## Dragon boat paddle terms

### Sting\+

Sting\+ is Hornet's upgraded adjustable dragon boat paddle platform. Graphic Sting\+ paddles use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, 3K twill carbon fiber weave, a matte finish, full-color graphics on both sides, Diamond Edge blade reinforcement, removable non-slip grip covers, and IDBF license 3525.

### STEALTH Performance Series

STEALTH Performance Series is Hornet's racing-focused dragon boat paddle family. STEALTH paddles use marbleized carbon construction, high-performance PMI foam cores, reinforced Diamond Edge blades, glossy performance finishes with metallic accents, removable non-slip grip covers, and IDBF license 3525.

### Clamp Adjustable

Clamp Adjustable is one of Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle handle options. It weighs approximately 16 oz and adjusts from 45 in to 51 in.

### Leverlock Adjustable

Leverlock Adjustable is one of Hornet's adjustable dragon boat paddle handle options. It weighs approximately 20 oz and adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.

### PMI foam core

PMI foam core is the inner core material used in Hornet's Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddle families.

### 3K carbon fiber

3K carbon fiber is the exterior construction used on Hornet's graphic Sting\+ paddles. Their construction also uses a 3K twill carbon fiber weave.

### Marbleized carbon construction

Marbleized carbon construction gives STEALTH Performance Series paddles their distinctive raw carbon appearance. The material pattern varies, so no two paddles look exactly alike.

### Diamond Edge

Diamond Edge is Hornet's proprietary reinforced blade edge used on Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series paddles. It is designed to help prevent chips and cracks over time.

### IDBF license 3525

Hornet's Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series adjustable dragon boat paddle families are IDBF approved under license 3525. Paddlers should still check event-specific rules before racing.

## Kayak paddle terms

### Hornet kayak paddle shared construction

Hornet kayak paddles share the same construction and specifications across the line. The graphic is the main difference between models.

### Carbon fiber and fiberglass kayak paddle

Hornet kayak paddles are made of carbon fiber and fiberglass. Hornet describes this construction as providing useful flex for both leisurely paddling and harder workouts.

### Adjustable kayak paddle shaft

Hornet kayak paddles adjust from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm.

### Blade rotation

Blade rotation is the adjustable angle between the paddle blades. Hornet kayak paddles support settings from 0 to 60 degrees.

### Blade feathering

Blade feathering offsets the blade angle. Hornet kayak paddles allow feathering in 15-degree increments.

### 2-piece kayak paddle

Hornet kayak paddles break down into 2 parts for easier storage and transport.

### Drip rings

Hornet kayak paddles include 2 drip rings.

### Direct-printed kayak paddle graphic

Hornet kayak paddle graphics are printed directly on the blade rather than applied as stickers.

## Accessory terms

### Paddle blade cover

A paddle blade cover protects the blade area. Hornet blade covers include a back zip pocket and are described as fitting IDBF-approved paddles.

### Paddle bag

A paddle bag protects the full paddle during storage or transport. Hornet's full paddle bags are described as protecting against scratches and damage and fitting up to two paddles.

### Dragon Guard

Dragon Guard is Hornet's dragon boat paddle tip protector.

### Silicone grip tape

Silicone grip tape is a self-fusing grip accessory used to create a more secure hold in wet paddling conditions.

### Paddle cleaner

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is intended for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

## Rowing terms

### Concept2 compatibility

Concept2 compatibility means a product is designed or described for use with a Concept2 rowing machine seat. Check the individual product page for size, fit, and model-specific details.
