## Dragon boat paddle Q&A drafts

### What Hornet dragon boat paddle should I start with?

Start with an active Hornet Sting\+ adjustable dragon boat paddle that has the handle option you want in stock. Compare fit, handle availability, verified specs, and design before choosing.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Paddle Buying Guide](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-paddle-buying-guide), [Shop Dragon Boat](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/collections/dragon-boat)

### What is a Sting\+ dragon boat paddle?

A Sting\+ dragon boat paddle is Hornet's upgraded adjustable dragon boat paddle platform. The verified graphic Sting\+ paddles use PMI foam cores, 3K carbon fiber exteriors, Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, Clamp or Leverlock adjustment, and IDBF license 3525.

Sources: [Clamp vs Leverlock](/dragon-boat/clamp-vs-leverlock-dragon-boat-paddles)

### Is Clamp or Leverlock better?

Hornet currently needs more verified source data before claiming that Clamp or Leverlock is better. Choose based on current product-page details, coach or team preference, and in-stock availability.

Sources: [Clamp vs Leverlock](/dragon-boat/clamp-vs-leverlock-dragon-boat-paddles)

### How should I choose a dragon boat paddle length?

Choose dragon boat paddle length based on paddler size, technique, coach guidance, and handle range. Hornet Aluminum Clamp adjusts from 45 in to 51 in, while Hornet Leverlock adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.

Sources: [How to Choose Dragon Boat Paddle Length](/dragon-boat/how-to-choose-dragon-boat-paddle-length)
