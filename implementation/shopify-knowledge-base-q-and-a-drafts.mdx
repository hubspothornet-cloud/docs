## Dragon boat accessory Q&A drafts

### What are the best Hornet accessories for dragon boat paddlers?

The best Hornet accessories depend on the problem: use a seat pad for comfort, grip tape for handling, Dragon Guard or a blade cover for protection, and a paddle bag for travel or storage.

Sources: [Best Dragon Boat Accessories](/dragon-boat/best-dragon-boat-accessories)

### Should I buy a dragon boat seat pad with a strap?

Buy the strapped dragon boat seat pad if you want the pad to wrap around the seat and include small-item storage. Choose the simpler non-slip seat pad if you want basic cushioned comfort and grip.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Seat Pads](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-seat-pads)

### What is the difference between Hornet's strapped and non-slip dragon boat seat pads?

Hornet's strapped seat pad adds a wraparound strap and storage pockets, while the non-slip seat pad is the simpler comfort and grip option. Check current colors and inventory on Shopify.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Seat Pads](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-seat-pads)

### Is the Dragon Boat Seat Pad with Strap IDBF approved?

The current Shopify copy says the Dragon Boat Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets is IDBF approved and meets IDBF standards. Race buyers should still confirm event requirements before buying.

Sources: [Seat Pad with Strap Facts](/dragon-boat/data/dragon-boat-seat-pad-with-strap)

### What does Dragon Guard do?

Dragon Guard is Hornet's dragon boat paddle tip protector. Current Shopify copy says it protects your dragon boat paddle, but exact material and compatibility details still need verification.

Sources: [Dragon Guard Facts](/dragon-boat/data/dragon-guard-tip-protector)

### What is Hornet Silicone Grip Tape for?

Hornet Silicone Grip Tape is a self-fusing grip accessory for paddlers who want a more secure hold in wet paddle sport conditions. It is also available in multiple colors.

Sources: [Silicone Grip Tape Facts](/dragon-boat/data/silicone-grip-tape-for-dragon-boat)

### Should I use a paddle bag or a blade cover?

Use a paddle bag when you want full-paddle storage or travel protection, and use a blade cover when you want blade-focused protection with less bulk. A tip protector adds smaller edge protection.

Sources: [Paddle Bags, Blade Covers, and Protection](/dragon-boat/paddle-bags-blade-covers-and-protection)

### Do Hornet dragon boat paddle bags fit more than one paddle?

The current Shopify copy for Hornet's full dragon boat paddle bags says they fit up to two paddles. Confirm current product details and dimensions on the Shopify product page before buying.

Sources: [Black Paddle Bag Facts](/dragon-boat/data/black-dragon-boat-paddle-bag), [Pink/Black Paddle Bag Facts](/dragon-boat/data/pink-black-dragon-boat-paddle-bag)

### What is the difference between a paddle bag and a blade cover?

A paddle bag is for full-paddle travel or storage, while a blade cover focuses on protecting the blade area. Hornet blade covers also list a back zip pocket in current Shopify copy.

Sources: [Paddle Bags, Blade Covers, and Protection](/dragon-boat/paddle-bags-blade-covers-and-protection)

### What paddles can Hornet Paddle Cleaner be used on?

Current Shopify copy says Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is described as ideal for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

Sources: [Paddle Cleaner Facts](/dragon-boat/data/paddle-cleaner-carbon-fiber-fiberglass-v2)

### How do I use Hornet Paddle Cleaner?

Current Shopify copy says to spray Hornet Paddle Cleaner and wipe with a soft cloth or sponge. For tougher stains, the copy says to let it sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.

Sources: [Paddle Cleaner Facts](/dragon-boat/data/paddle-cleaner-carbon-fiber-fiberglass-v2)
