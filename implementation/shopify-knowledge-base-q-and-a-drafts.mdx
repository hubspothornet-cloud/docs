## Dragon boat paddle Q&A

### What is a Hornet Sting\+ dragon boat paddle?

A Hornet Sting\+ is an IDBF-approved adjustable dragon boat paddle platform with a PMI foam core, carbon construction, Diamond Edge blade reinforcement, a removable non-slip grip cover, and Clamp or Leverlock handle options.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Paddle Buying Guide](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-paddle-buying-guide)

### What is the Hornet STEALTH Performance Series?

The STEALTH Performance Series is Hornet's racing-focused adjustable dragon boat paddle family. It uses marbleized carbon construction, a high-performance PMI foam core, Diamond Edge blade reinforcement, a glossy finish, and IDBF license 3525.

Sources: [Why Choose Hornet Paddles](/dragon-boat/why-choose-hornet-dragon-boat-paddles)

### Are Hornet dragon boat paddles IDBF approved?

Yes. Hornet Sting\+ graphic and STEALTH Performance Series adjustable paddles are IDBF approved under license 3525. Paddlers should still confirm event-specific rules with their race organizer or team.

Sources: [IDBF Approved Dragon Boat Paddles](/dragon-boat/idbf-approved-dragon-boat-paddles)

### What is the difference between Hornet Clamp and Leverlock paddles?

Clamp weighs approximately 16 oz and adjusts from 45 in to 51 in. Leverlock weighs approximately 20 oz and adjusts from 43 in to 51 in.

Sources: [Clamp vs Leverlock](/dragon-boat/clamp-vs-leverlock-dragon-boat-paddles)

### Which Hornet handle is lighter?

Clamp is the lighter Hornet adjustable handle option at approximately 16 oz. Leverlock weighs approximately 20 oz.

Sources: [Clamp vs Leverlock](/dragon-boat/clamp-vs-leverlock-dragon-boat-paddles)

### Which Hornet handle has the lower starting range?

Leverlock starts at 43 in, while Aluminum Clamp starts at 45 in. Both adjust up to 51 in.

Sources: [How to Choose Dragon Boat Paddle Length](/dragon-boat/how-to-choose-dragon-boat-paddle-length)

### What is the best Hornet dragon boat paddle for beginners?

An adjustable Hornet paddle is a strong starting point for beginners because the length can be refined as technique develops. Choose Clamp or Leverlock based on fit, weight preference, coach guidance, and availability.

Sources: [Best Paddles by Paddler Type](/dragon-boat/best-hornet-dragon-boat-paddles-by-paddler-type)

### What is the best Hornet dragon boat paddle for high-level racing?

Start with the STEALTH Performance Series when high-level racing is the priority. STEALTH emphasizes marbleized carbon construction, stiffness, responsiveness, durability, and IDBF approval.

Sources: [Best Paddles by Paddler Type](/dragon-boat/best-hornet-dragon-boat-paddles-by-paddler-type)

### Are Hornet paddles only about artwork?

No. Hornet paddles use distinctive graphics and finishes, but the adjustable families also include PMI foam cores, carbon construction, Diamond Edge blades, removable non-slip grip covers, defined adjustable ranges, and IDBF approval.

Sources: [Why Choose Hornet Paddles](/dragon-boat/why-choose-hornet-dragon-boat-paddles)

### Should I choose a Sting\+ graphic paddle or STEALTH paddle?

Choose Sting\+ graphic paddles for full-color artwork with 3K carbon fiber construction. Choose STEALTH for a racing-focused paddle with marbleized carbon construction and a glossy performance finish.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Paddle Buying Guide](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-paddle-buying-guide)

## Dragon boat accessory Q&A

Choose the Non-Slip Seat Pad for simple comfort and grip. Choose the Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets for a wraparound fit, phone holder, mesh pocket, and carrying handle.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Seat Pads](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-seat-pads)

### Are Hornet dragon boat seat pads IDBF approved?

Both Hornet seat pad product pages include IDBF approval language. Paddlers should still confirm event-specific rules before racing.

Sources: [Dragon Boat Seat Pads](/dragon-boat/dragon-boat-seat-pads)

### What size is the Hornet strapped dragon boat seat pad?

The Seat Pad with Secure Strap and Storage Pockets measures 12 in x 6 in x 0.6 in.

Sources: [Shop Seat Pad with Strap](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-boat-seat-pad-with-strap)

### What does Dragon Guard do?

Dragon Guard is Hornet's dragon boat paddle tip protector. It can be used alone or with a paddle bag or blade cover for additional paddle protection.

Sources: [Shop Dragon Guard](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/dragon-guard)

### What is Hornet Silicone Grip Tape for?

Hornet Silicone Grip Tape is a self-fusing grip accessory used to create a more secure hold in wet paddling conditions. It is available in multiple colors.

Sources: [Shop Silicone Grip Tape](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/silicone-grip-tape-for-dragon-boat)

### Should I use a paddle bag or a blade cover?

Use a paddle bag for full-paddle storage and transport. Use a blade cover for blade-focused protection with less bulk.

Sources: [Paddle Bags, Blade Covers, and Protection](/dragon-boat/paddle-bags-blade-covers-and-protection)

### Do Hornet full paddle bags fit more than one paddle?

Hornet's Black and Pink/Black full paddle bags are described as fitting up to two paddles.

Sources: [Paddle Bags, Blade Covers, and Protection](/dragon-boat/paddle-bags-blade-covers-and-protection)

### Do Hornet paddle blade covers have storage pockets?

Yes. Hornet blade covers include a back zip pocket for small items such as a phone, keys, or wallet.

Sources: [Paddle Bags, Blade Covers, and Protection](/dragon-boat/paddle-bags-blade-covers-and-protection)

### What paddles can Hornet Paddle Cleaner be used on?

Hornet Paddle Cleaner is designed for carbon fiber, fiberglass, composite, and plastic paddles. It is intended for dragon boat, kayak, canoe, and outrigger paddles.

Sources: [Shop Paddle Cleaner](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/paddle-cleaner-safe-for-carbon-fiber-fiberglass-marine-safe-made-in-usa)

### How do I use Hornet Paddle Cleaner?

Spray the cleaner and wipe with a soft cloth or sponge. For tougher stains, let it sit for 60 to 90 seconds before wiping.

Sources: [Shop Paddle Cleaner](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/products/paddle-cleaner-safe-for-carbon-fiber-fiberglass-marine-safe-made-in-usa)

Hornet kayak paddles adjust from 90.5 in to 94.5 in, or 230 cm to 240 cm.

## Rowing machine Q&A

### Which Hornet rowing cushion should I choose for Concept2?

Choose the Anti Slip Cushion for grip and Regular or XL sizing, the Memory Foam Cushion for thicker padding, or the Concept2 Seat Cushion for the strongest snug-fit positioning.

Sources: [Best Concept2 Seat Cushions](/rowing-machine/best-rowing-machine-seat-cushions-for-concept2)

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has an XL option?

The Anti Slip Rowing Machine Cushion has Regular and XL variants.

Sources: [Rowing Machine Seat Cushion Buying Guide](/rowing-machine/rowing-machine-seat-cushion-buying-guide)

### Which Hornet rowing cushion is thickest?

Hornet describes the Memory Foam Cushion Made for Concept2 as its thickest version and almost 2 inches thick.

Sources: [Memory Foam vs Anti-Slip](/rowing-machine/memory-foam-vs-anti-slip-rowing-seat-cushions)

### What is the difference between memory foam and anti-slip rowing cushions?

Choose memory foam for thicker padding. Choose the anti-slip cushion for grip and Regular or XL sizing.

Sources: [Memory Foam vs Anti-Slip](/rowing-machine/memory-foam-vs-anti-slip-rowing-seat-cushions)

### Is a rowing seat pad the same as a rowing machine cushion?

Not always. A rowing seat pad is usually a simpler anti-slip comfort layer, while a rowing machine cushion may be designed around machine fit, thickness, or size options.

Sources: [Rowing Seat Pad vs Rowing Machine Cushion](/rowing-machine/rowing-seat-pad-vs-rowing-machine-cushion)

### Can a rowing cushion fix pain?

No. Rowing cushions are comfort accessories, not medical treatment or guaranteed solutions for pain, numbness, injury, or technique problems.

Sources: [How to Reduce Rowing Machine Seat Discomfort](/rowing-machine/how-to-reduce-rowing-machine-seat-discomfort)

## Gift Q&A

## Policy Q&A

### What is Hornet's dragon boat paddle warranty?

Hornet provides a 3-year warranty on dragon boat paddles from the original purchase date. The warranty covers manufacturing defects, not damage from use, impact, normal wear, misuse, handling, or transportation.

Sources: [Warranty and Shipping](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/warranty)

### What is Hornet's warranty on other products?

Hornet provides a 1-year warranty on products other than dragon boat paddles from the original purchase date.

Sources: [Warranty and Shipping](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/warranty)

### What is Hornet's return window?

Unused USA purchases made through Hornetwatersports.com may be returned or exchanged within 30 days of purchase with prior authorization. International purchases are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

Sources: [Returns and Exchanges](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/returns-exchanges)

### Are clearance items returnable?

No. Hornet clearance items are final sale and are not eligible for return, refund, or exchange.

Sources: [Returns and Exchanges](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/returns-exchanges)

### How quickly do in-stock Hornet orders ship?

Hornet states that in-stock orders ship by USPS or UPS within 1 to 2 business days after receipt, Monday through Friday.

Sources: [Warranty and Shipping](https://www.hornetwatersports.com/pages/warranty)
