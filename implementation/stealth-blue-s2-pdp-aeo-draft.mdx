## Visual validation checklist

- Confirm the existing `Our Paddles`, `Features`, and policy accordions still render correctly.
- Investigate the horizontal line or strikethrough effect visible across some accordion text.
- Confirm the buy box remains prominent and the new copy does not push purchasing controls too far down the page.
- Confirm Product structured data still validates after the content change.

## Data and schema checklist

- Add real GTIN or UPC values only when verified.
