Use this template before writing product-specific buyer guides. The goal is to separate verified product facts from marketing copy, so future guide pages do not invent specs or copy stale information.

## Template

```mdx
---
title: "[Product Name] Facts"
description: "Verified product facts for [Product Name] from Hornet Watersports."
---

## Product summary

[One or two sentences describing what the product is, who it is for, and the primary use case.]

## Verified facts

Product type:  
Sport or use case:  
Primary material:  
Compatibility:  
Size or dimensions:  
Weight:  
Color or design options:  
Included items:  
Care instructions:  
Warranty:  
Shopify product URL:  

## Facts needing verification

- [Missing spec]
- [Missing compatibility claim]
- [Missing warranty or return detail]

## Do not claim yet

- [Any claim that is not supported by current product data]
```

## Rule

If a product-specific fact is not in the Shopify product export, product page, internal spec sheet, or verified source, leave it blank and flag it for review. Do not invent the value.
