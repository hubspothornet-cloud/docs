## Quick answer

Choose Hornet paddling gloves when you want fingerless gloves with silicone grip on the palm side to help reduce slipping and add security while paddling or rowing.

## Why paddling gloves matter

Paddling and rowing can be hard on the hands, especially in wet conditions, long practices, races, or repeated indoor workouts. Gloves help protect the hands and support a more secure hold on the paddle or rowing handle.
