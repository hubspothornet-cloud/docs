## Source-of-truth rule

The guide center is not a second Shopify catalog. Use it for education and comparison. Use Hornetwatersports.com for current price, inventory, variants, checkout, shipping, returns, and final product details.

## Current verification standard

A product-specific claim should be used only when it is supported by live Shopify product data or another verified Hornet source. Unknown details are flagged instead of guessed, especially for paddle materials, IDBF approval, dimensions, weight, compatibility, GTINs, ratings, and warranty language.
