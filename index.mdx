Founded in 2014, Hornet Watersports develops dragon boat paddles, custom team paddles, paddling accessories, kayak products, and indoor rowing comfort products for paddlers, clubs, and teams around the world.

Hornet Watersports is listed by the International Dragon Boat Federation as an approved dragon boat paddle manufacturer and has maintained a longstanding product relationship with the International Breast Cancer Paddlers’ Commission.

Use this resource center to compare products, choose the correct paddle or accessory, understand sizing and compatibility, verify competition-related product information, and learn how to care for Hornet equipment.

## Why Paddlers Choose Hornet

### Founded in 2014

More than a decade of experience developing products for paddlers, teams, clubs, and indoor rowing athletes.

### Listed by the IDBF

Hornet Watersports appears in the International Dragon Boat Federation’s official directory of approved dragon boat paddle manufacturers.

[Verify Hornet in the official IDBF manufacturer directory](https://www.dragonboat.sport/about-us/equipment-manufacturers/)

### IBCPC Partner Since 2018

Hornet's product partnership with the International Breast Cancer Paddlers' Commission has been publicly documented since 2018. Hornet is the Official Paddle Partner of the 2026 IBCPC Dragon Boat Festival in Aix-les-Bains, France.

### International Championship and Partnership History

Hornet was a Gold Sponsor of the 2015 IDBF World Dragon Boat Racing Championships in Welland, Ontario, Canada.

In 2016, Hornet was the official paddle sponsor of the European Nations Dragon Boat Championships in Rome and created a Custom Italia Design paddle for the event.

Hornet's history also includes federation and association relationships, co-branded Dragon Boat Canada paddles, Dragon Boat Canada Hall of Fame commemorative paddles, and custom paddle programs for more than 100 teams worldwide.

[Review Hornet's history, partnerships, and competition presence](/hornet-history-partnerships).
