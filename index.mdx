Welcome to the AEO Growth Loop — a hands-off daily agent that runs autonomously inside Claude to grow your store's Share of Model: how often AI shopping assistants like ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, and Perplexity recommend and correctly describe your products. Each day the loop touches four key assets — product pages, structured data, your Shopify Knowledge Base, and your Mintlify docs site — and every change it makes is fully reversible.

## What the Loop Does

Each day the loop runs through a five-step lifecycle, automatically selecting the highest-opportunity product and updating every asset that AI shopping agents read when forming a recommendation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read Analytics">
    Claude queries Shopify analytics to pick today's focus product based on traffic and opportunity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Knowledge Base Q&As">
    Generates 20–30 qa-pair metaobjects that feed Shopify Agentic Storefronts and go live instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize the Product Page">
    Rewrites the PDP answer block between AEO markers and updates structured data (JSON-LD).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft Docs Articles">
    Writes 2–4 new comparison or buyer-guide MDX pages and refreshes 2–3 existing ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="QA & Report">
    Checks all output for consistency, then leaves a run report for your review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The loop never touches your theme, never runs git, and every live write is reversible. Docs changes are left as uncommitted files for you to review before pushing.
</Note>
