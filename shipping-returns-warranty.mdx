## Warranty

Hornet states that dragon boat paddles are covered by a 3 year warranty and all other products are covered by a 1 year warranty. Warranty coverage applies to manufacturing defects, not damage from use.

For warranty claims, Hornet asks customers to send product photos and details to `info@hornetwatersports.com`. The current warranty page also lists a handling fee for warranty claims: USD 25 for USA claims and USD 50 for international claims.

## Returns and exchanges

Hornet accepts returns and exchanges of unused items purchased through Hornetwatersports.com within 30 days of the purchase date. Items must be unused, in new condition, and returned in the original box or packaging with tags if applicable.

All returns require a Return Authorization. Customers should contact `info@hornetwatersports.com` with their order number and return details before sending anything back.

The current return policy says customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Refunds are applied to the original method of payment for merchandise only, and shipping charges are not refunded.

<Warning>
  Clearance items are final sale and are not eligible for return, refund, or exchange.
</Warning>

## Shipping

Hornet states that orders for in-stock items ship by USPS or UPS within 1 to 2 business days after the order is received. Orders ship Monday through Friday, and orders placed on weekends or holidays ship the next business day.

Customers should contact `info@hornetwatersports.com` within 24 hours for requested order changes. Hornet says it will do its best to accommodate change requests, and express shipping is available upon request.
