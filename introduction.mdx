The AEO Growth Loop is a daily autonomous agent that runs inside Claude and optimizes your Shopify store for AI shopping agents. AEO stands for **Answer Engine Optimization** — the practice of making your products discoverable, accurately described, and consistently recommended by AI engines like ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, and Perplexity. The loop runs once a day, touches four key assets across your store and docs site, and compounds in effectiveness over time.

## The Problem It Solves

AI shopping agents read different signals than traditional search engines. Instead of crawling backlinks and keyword density, they rely on structured Q&A pairs, rich product descriptions, JSON-LD schema, and long-form buyer guides to build their understanding of a product. Most Shopify stores aren't optimized for these signals — so even great products get ignored entirely or misdescribed when an AI agent fields a buying question.

The AEO Growth Loop fixes this systematically. Every day it writes new Q&A pairs to your Knowledge Base, sharpens your product descriptions for AI citation, keeps your structured data complete and accurate, and publishes the buyer-guide content that AI engines prefer to quote. No manual editing required.

## What's Share of Model?

Share of Model is how often AI agents correctly recommend and describe your products when a shopper asks a relevant question. Unlike an SEO ranking, it isn't determined by a single algorithm — it's driven by the **quality, completeness, and consistency** of the information AI engines have indexed about you across your store, your docs site, and the structured data embedded in your pages.

A higher Share of Model means more AI-referred shoppers arriving with accurate expectations, higher intent, and lower return rates. For a deeper dive, see the [Share of Model concept page](/concepts/share-of-model).

## Who this is for

The AEO Growth Loop is built for Shopify store owners who want to grow agentic traffic without manually editing every product page. It works across any product vertical and ships with YMYL guardrails for stores selling health products, devices, or supplements — categories where AI agents apply stricter accuracy standards.

If you're already getting organic traffic but seeing little to no referrals from AI assistants, or if you've noticed AI agents describing your products inaccurately, the loop is designed to fix that steadily over weeks, not months.

<Tip>
  The loop is designed to be left running. A typical review takes 5 minutes per day — glance at the report, commit the docs changes, check the Agentic Storefronts panel.
</Tip>
