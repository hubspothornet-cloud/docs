Each daily run of the AEO Growth Loop improves four types of assets — the specific content formats that AI shopping agents read, parse, and cite when forming recommendations. Understanding what each asset does helps you evaluate the quality of the loop's output.

## 1. Shopify Product Pages (PDPs)

AI agents read product descriptions as prose, not marketing copy. The loop rewrites the description to lead with the answer a buyer needs: what the product does, who it's for, and what makes it distinct.

The rewrite happens inside `<!-- AEO:START -->` and `<!-- AEO:END -->` HTML comment markers. Everything outside those markers — your original content, images, tabs — is never touched.

The answer block follows a consistent structure: a lead paragraph, a spec table, an FAQ section, and (where applicable) a comparison note.

<Note>
  The loop backs up your full `descriptionHtml` before every write. Any run is reversible.
</Note>

## 2. Structured Data / Schema

JSON-LD schema is the most direct signal to AI search agents. The loop generates and maintains three schema types:

- **`Product`** — name, description, SKU, GTIN, brand
- **`Offer`** — price, availability, currency
- **`FAQPage`** — mirroring the PDP FAQ section

These are stored as `aeo.*` metafields on each product. A Liquid snippet you install once into your theme reads these metafields and injects the JSON-LD into the page `<head>` automatically. Once installed, all future loop runs update the values without any further theme work.

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Product",
  "name": "Magnesium Complex 300mg",
  "sku": "MAG-300-90",
  "brand": { "@type": "Brand", "name": "SonoHealth" },
  "offers": {
    "@type": "Offer",
    "price": "29.99",
    "priceCurrency": "USD",
    "availability": "https://schema.org/InStock"
  }
}
```

## 3. Shopify Knowledge Base

The Shopify Knowledge Base is powered by `qa-pair` metaobjects. Each metaobject contains a question and answer. Shopify's Agentic Storefronts feature surfaces these directly to AI shopping agents querying your store.

The loop writes 20–30 new Q&As per day for the focus product, based on actual buyer questions derived from analytics, competitor patterns, and product data. Q&As go live instantly — no push or deployment needed.

<Tip>
  After a few weeks of runs, your Knowledge Base will have hundreds of grounded, well-sourced Q&As. This is the asset with the fastest impact on agentic traffic.
</Tip>

## 4. Mintlify Docs Site

Long-form comparison and buyer-guide content is the format AI engines cite most often. A well-structured `docs.yourstore.com` filled with honest, detailed articles becomes a trusted source in AI training and retrieval pipelines.

The loop writes 2–4 new MDX articles per run and refreshes 2–3 existing ones. Article types include: product comparisons, buyer guides, use-case explainers, and FAQ compilations.

Changes are left as uncommitted files for you to review before pushing. Mintlify auto-deploys on every push.

<Note>
  Every article the loop writes is grounded in your `data.mdx` product facts pages. If a spec or claim isn't in the facts page, it's omitted and flagged — never invented.
</Note>
