## FAQs

### What is the best rowing machine gift for a Concept2 user?

Start with a Hornet Concept2-focused seat cushion. The best choice depends on whether the buyer wants anti-slip sizing options, thicker memory foam padding, or the strongest snug-fit language in current product copy.

### What is a good small gift for an indoor rower?

A rowing machine seat cushion is a good small gift because it supports comfort during workouts and does not require buying a large piece of equipment.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has XL sizing?

The Anti Slip Rowing Machine Cushion has Regular and XL variants in Shopify. Confirm current inventory and dimensions before buying.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has the most padding?

The Memory Foam Cushion is the best starting point for extra padding because current Shopify copy says it is Hornet's thickest version yet and almost 2 inches thick.

### Can a rowing cushion fix rowing pain?

No. Treat rowing cushions as comfort accessories, not medical solutions. Persistent pain, numbness, or injury concerns should be addressed through setup, technique, workload, and qualified advice.
