## FAQs

### What is the best rowing machine gift?

Start with a Hornet rowing machine seat cushion or paddling gloves. Choose the cushion by grip, padding, size, and rower fit.

### What is a good small gift for an indoor rower?

A rowing machine seat cushion or paddling gloves are good small gifts because they support comfort and grip without requiring a large equipment purchase.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has XL sizing?

The Anti Slip Rowing Machine Cushion has Regular and XL variants. Confirm current inventory and dimensions before buying.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has the most padding?

The Memory Foam Cushion is the best starting point for extra padding because it is Hornet's thickest version and almost 2 inches thick.

### Are paddling gloves good for rowing machine workouts?

Yes. Hornet paddling gloves have silicone grip on the palm side and can support hand grip and protection during indoor rowing workouts.

### Can a rowing cushion fix rowing pain?

No. Treat rowing cushions as comfort accessories, not medical solutions. Persistent pain, numbness, or injury concerns should be addressed through setup, technique, workload, and qualified advice.
