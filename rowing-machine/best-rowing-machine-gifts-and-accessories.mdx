## FAQs

### What is a good rowing machine gift for a Concept2 user?

Choose among the Hornet Concept2-focused cushions based on the recipient's priority: snug fit, anti-slip sizing options, or thicker memory foam padding.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has XL sizing?

The Anti Slip Rowing Machine Cushion has Regular and XL variants.

### Which Hornet rowing cushion has the most padding?

Hornet describes the Memory Foam Cushion Made for Concept2 as its thickest version and almost 2 inches thick.

### What is the safest gift when I do not know the machine or seat size?

A Hornet Gift Card is the safest option because the recipient can choose the correct product and size.

### Can a rowing cushion fix rowing pain?

No. Treat rowing cushions as comfort accessories. Persistent pain, numbness, or injury concerns should be addressed through setup, technique, workload, and qualified advice.
