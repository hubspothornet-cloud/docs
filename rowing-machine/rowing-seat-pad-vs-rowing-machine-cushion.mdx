## When is a simple rowing seat pad enough?

A simple seat pad is the better starting point when you want a basic comfort layer and anti-slip function, especially if you are not trying to solve a machine-specific fit question. The [Rowing and Sculling Anti Slip Seat Pad](/rowing-machine/data/rowing-and-sculling-anti-slip-seat-pad) is the current Hornet product in this guide set that fits that role.

Do not assume it fits Concept2 machines unless that compatibility is verified on the product page or internal source data.

## FAQs

### Is a rowing seat pad the same as a rowing machine cushion?

Not always. A rowing seat pad is usually a simpler comfort layer, while a rowing machine cushion may be designed around a specific machine seat, thickness, or size option.

### Which Hornet product should I choose for Concept2?
